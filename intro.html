Welcome to Your Cosmic Companion

The Psychedelic Joint Counter is not just an app; it's a portal to self-awareness and introspection. Whether you're exploring altered states of consciousness or simply looking for a unique way to track your thoughts and experiences, this tool is designed to be your digital confidant on your journey of self-discovery.

What It's Good For

  • Tracking consumption patterns and their effects on your mental state
  • Documenting moments of clarity and personal insights
  • Collecting and preserving valuable thoughts or ideas ("jewels")
  • Monitoring your mood fluctuations over time
  • Encouraging mindfulness and self-reflection

How to Use the Psychedelic Joint Counter

1. Counters

At the top of the app, you'll find three counters:

  • Joint Counter: Click "Add Joint" each time you consume. This helps track your usage patterns.
  • Clarity Moments: Hit "Add Clarity" when you experience a moment of heightened awareness or insight.
  • Jewels Collected: Press "Add Jewel" to record particularly valuable thoughts or ideas that come to you.

2. Cosmic Insights (Notes)

Use the "Cosmic Insights" section to record your thoughts, experiences, or revelations:

  1. Click in the text area to start typing.
  2. Use the formatting buttons (Bold, Italic, etc.) to style your text if desired.
  3. Click "Save Insight" to store your note.
  4. Your saved insights will appear below, where you can review, edit, or delete them later.

These stats help you understand your patterns and progress over time.

Tips for a Cosmic Experience

  • Use the app regularly to build a comprehensive record of your journey.
  • Be honest and open in your notes - this is your personal space for reflection.
  • Pay attention to correlations between your consumption, mood, and insights.
  • Review your past entries periodically to track your growth and recurring themes.
  • Remember, the app is a tool for self-discovery - let it guide you, but trust your own intuition above all.
